对牛弹琴

(duì niú tán qín)

成语解释

  • An ancient Chinese musician came to the outskirts, Sitting on the ground playing the guqin, Guqin is a traditional Chinese plucked string instrument,laid across the ground, The sound of the qin is beautiful and pleasant to listen, There is a cow near him, The cow ignored him and lowered its head to eat grass.
